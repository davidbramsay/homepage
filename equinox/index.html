Equinox

Capturing Naturalistic Time Distortion



David developed this smartwatch platform to study how people experience time compression or expansion in daily life. The watch works by asking people to guess the time whenever they would like to check the time. The watch also captures ambient light levels, temperature, and humidity data to help inform a model of time perception.



This work is under review; the data is very interesting. Please contact David for more information.
